## 🚫 COMMON SEO MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **❌ Keyword Stuffing**
   - Don't repeat keywords unnaturally
   - Write for humans first, search engines second
   - Use variations and synonyms

2. **❌ Duplicate Content**
   - Don't copy content from other websites
   - Create unique product descriptions
   - Don't duplicate your own content across pages

3. **❌ Ignoring Mobile Users**
   - 70%+ of Kenya searches are mobile
   - Test your site on mobile devices
   - Ensure fast loading on mobile networks

4. **❌ Buying Fake Reviews**
   - Google detects and penalizes this
   - Get genuine reviews from real customers
   - Quality over quantity

5. **❌ Neglecting Google Business Profile**
   - This is your #1 local SEO asset
   - Update weekly with posts, photos, offers
   - Respond to all reviews

6. **❌ Slow Website**
   - Compress images
   - Use caching
   - Choose good hosting
   - Target: <3 second load time

7. **❌ No Local Content**
   - Always mention Rongai, Nairobi, Kenya
   - Create location-specific content
   - Use local landmarks and references

8. **❌ Inconsistent NAP**
   - Name, Address, Phone must match everywhere
   - Use same format across all platforms
   - Inconsistency confuses search engines
